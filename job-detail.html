Senior UI Designer

Tokopedia

  • Fulltime

  • Jakarta, Indonesia

  • Dirahasiakan

Job Descriptions

  • Collaborate and give advise to UX Designers & UX Engineers on UI perspective from initial product design process
  • Present the outstanding design with user's need in mind
  • Build prototype and do design testing
  • Build UI Component on Design System
  • Create pixel perfect design implementation
  • Determine the interface style, best practices and standards
  • Maintain quality of UI output after development
  • Maintain design backlog with Product Development Team (Designers, Engineers and Product Owners)
  • Proactively improve and develop new process/system with a good balance between new trends and product requirements

Qualifications

  • Minimum 3 (three) years experience as UI Designer
  • Bachelor's Degree from Visual Communication Design/ related field, but impresive portfolio matters more
  • Deep understanding of design process
  • Excellent with design tools (such as Figma, Photoshop, Illustrator) and Prototyping tools ( such as InVision, Marvel, etc.)
  • Experience in animation,illustration making, visual gamification interface
  • Deep Understanding Android & iOS behavior
  • Good typography skills, structural design and information architecture
  • Deep understanding about company brand
  • Team player, experienced in working with agile teams
  • Excellent problem solving skill, especially on balancing design aesthetic and product needs
  • Capable to set design direction
  • Abilityto communicate well in English, both written and verbally
